About This Project

This interactive mapping application provides detailed information on the vulnerability factors of COVID-19 in the counties in the United States. Combining socioeconomic statistics and public health indicators, we enable researchers, policymakers and the public to investigate the regional differences in pandemic effects and preparedness.

The map integrates various datasets to understand the most important indicators, such as poverty rates, vaccination rates and COVID-19 case incidence, enabling to comparative analysis and identification of the vulnerable¬†areas.

How to Use the Interactive Map

  • Layer Controls: Use the checkboxes in the top-right corner to toggle different datasets on/off
  • Navigation: Zoom with mouse wheel and pan by clicking/dragging the map
  • Detailed Information: Click on any county to view detailed popup information
  • Multiple Views: Compare different vulnerability factors by showing/hiding layers

Data Sources & Methodology

This visualization integrates data from the US Census Bureau, CDC vaccination records, and state health department case reports. All data is normalized and aggregated at the county level for consistent comparison.
